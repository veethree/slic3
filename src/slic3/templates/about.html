What is slic3?

Slic3 is a small utility that takes long links and shortens them to make them more sharable!

It can turn this garbage:

{{ example_link }}

To this beauty:

{{ my_url }}{{ example_short }}

About slic3 links

Slic3 links aren't permanent. They stop working after 24 hours, Unless their timestamp is refreshed by shortening the same URL. They are also ALL public. Anyone can see a full list of all currently active links on the

Active links

page.
